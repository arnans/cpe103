How Your Grade Works

Your grade is built from the five course outcomes (CLO1–CLO5). For each one you aim to reach:

  • Mastery — you did exactly what the outcome asks. This is the target.
  • Exemplary — you went beyond it (on the Final Project, or Lab 7 for CLO4).

Meet the target on all five outcomes and you earn a B. Exemplary work is what lifts you to B+ and A.

  • Each CLO score is the average of its criteria.
  • Your final score (out of 4) is the average of your five CLO scores.
